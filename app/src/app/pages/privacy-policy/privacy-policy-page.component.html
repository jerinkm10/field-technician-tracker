{{ appName }} Privacy Policy

This policy explains how High Cooling Solutions collects, uses, stores, and protects information when technicians, employees, administrators, and business users use the field service mobile application, admin dashboard, and related API services.

Effective date {{ effectiveDate }}
Published URL {{ publicUrl }}

Summary

The app is used to manage cooling service operations, technician assignments, live field visibility, customer service records, invoices, AMC records, leads, complaints, and reports.

  • Location data is used for field tracking and job coordination only when permission is granted.
  • Account and business records are used to operate the service and protect authorized access.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • The production API is served over HTTPS at https://star.castorwheel.co.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to the High Cooling Solutions mobile application, admin dashboard, and backend services made available at star.castorwheel.co. It applies to technicians, employees, administrators, branch users, customers whose records are managed in the system, and other users who access the service.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on your role and the features used, we may collect or process:

  • Account details such as name, username, phone number, email address, role, and status.
  • Authentication and session information needed to sign in and protect the service.
  • Technician location information, including approximate or precise location, when the mobile app has location permission and tracking or job-related features are active.
  • Job, task, attendance, complaint, lead, AMC, invoice, quotation, customer, branch, and report records entered by authorized users.
  • Customer and business contact information such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, billing addresses, shipping addresses, service addresses, GST details, and service notes where those records are entered into the system.
  • Technical information such as device type, IP address, API request details, error logs, app version, and diagnostic data used to maintain security and reliability.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and provide role-based access to the app and dashboard.
  • Assign, manage, track, and report on technician jobs and field activity.
  • Show authorized administrators live or recent technician location information.
  • Maintain customer, complaint, AMC, lead, billing, invoice, quotation, and ledger records.
  • Send operational updates, support users, troubleshoot issues, and improve service reliability.
  • Protect the service against unauthorized access, misuse, fraud, and security incidents.
  • Meet business, accounting, legal, tax, and compliance obligations.

4. Location Data

The technician mobile application may request location permission so High Cooling Solutions can coordinate field work, view technician availability, validate service visits, and improve dispatch decisions. Location data is visible only to authorized users with a legitimate operational need.

Users can control location access through device permission settings. If location permission is disabled, some tracking, live map, dispatch, and job verification features may not work as intended.

5. Sharing And Disclosure

We may share information only as needed for the purposes described in this policy:

  • With authorized High Cooling Solutions administrators, employees, and branch users.
  • With service providers who host, maintain, secure, or support the application.
  • With professional advisers, auditors, or authorities when required by law or valid process.
  • During a business transfer, merger, restructuring, or similar organizational change.

We do not sell personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as it is needed to provide the service, maintain business and accounting records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, protect the service, and comply with legal obligations. Location, job, customer, and billing records may be retained as part of operational history unless deletion is required and permitted.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information, including authenticated access, role-based authorization, HTTPS for production API requests, and operational monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so users should protect their login credentials and report suspected misuse.

8. User Choices And Rights

Users may request access, correction, update, or deletion of personal information where applicable. Technicians may manage location permission from their device settings. Some requests may be limited where records must be retained for business, legal, tax, security, or operational reasons.

9. Children

The service is intended for business operations and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this service.

10. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published at {{ publicUrl }} with the effective date shown above.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, account requests, or data-related requests, contact the High Cooling Solutions administrator who issued your account or use the official developer contact provided in the app store listing.
